About Us

Our Mission

To advocate for environmental justice across Africa through innovative litigation, community empowerment, and sustainable solutions.

Our History

Founded in 2006 by Dr. Mandla Kawewe in Lusaka after witnessing a poisoned river, ZIEJ has grown into a leading voice for environmental rights.

Our Team

A dedicated group of 130 professionals, including lawyers, scientists, and activists, committed to environmental justice.

Our Impact

Secured over $18M in reparations and launched the 'Air for All' platform, impacting millions across the continent.
